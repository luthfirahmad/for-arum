🌸 Body Changes

What your body is going through

All normal — you're doing amazing, seriously.

😮‍💨
Shortness of Breath Normal
Hormones + your growing womb pressing up. If it gets so bad it limits your movement, see the doctor right away.
🤧
Stuffy Nose Normal
Hormone-related. Use a saline nasal spray or inhaler (like Plossa) for relief — totally safe during pregnancy.
🩸
Vaginal Discharge Normal
Safe as long as it looks and smells normal. Use a panty liner for comfort — no internal washing needed, it'll clean itself.
💓
Blood Pressure Rise Normal
A slight increase is expected as your blood volume grows. Keep up with your prenatal checkups.
⚖️
Weight Gain Normal
Don't panic — but don't use it as an excuse to eat everything either 😅 Swap ice cream for smoothies or healthy snacks to keep pace steady.
👗
Time to Buy Maternity Clothes
Only buy when your regular clothes truly don't fit anymore — and buy little by little since sizes keep changing.

Tips: Dark colors · Solid tops & bottoms · Small or vertical prints (no wide horizontal stripes) · True-to-size fit, not too loose or too tight.
🧠
Forgetfulness Normal
Brain cells literally shrink during pregnancy — it's a real thing! Eat protein + complex carbs, sleep well, and lean on me to remind you of things 🤍
You're growing a human, give yourself grace 🤍 Month 4
📋 Daily Reminders

Things to keep up with every day

Small habits, big impact for you and baby.

Daily Care
🍊
Eat Vitamin C-rich food
Oranges, strawberries, bell peppers, broccoli — pick your fave daily!
🪥
Brush teeth — gently
Gums are extra sensitive now. Go slow, soft bristle only.
🚿
Use the water flosser
Kinder on your gums than regular floss — use it daily.
Movement & Exercise
🚶‍♀️
Move at least 30 min/day
Walking, swimming, prenatal yoga, or pregnancy aerobics — 4x/week minimum. Ask your doctor which is best for you. No lying flat on your back. Always warm up & cool down.
⏱️
Walk 5–10 min every hour
If you sit all day at work, get up every hour or your ankles will swell. I'll remind you 🙂
Basic Pregnancy Exercises (Tap to expand)
01Shoulder Stretch
▼
Sit or stand tall. Roll shoulders backward in big, slow circles — down, back, up, forward. Keep breathing throughout. Then reverse direction.
⏱ 2–4 sets · 8–10 circles each direction
02Standing Leg Stretch
▼
Stand facing a wall with hands on it. Step one foot back, toes forward. Keep back heel on floor, front knee bent. Lean hips forward until you feel a calf stretch. Hold, then switch.
⏱ 2–4 sets · Hold 15–30 sec each leg
03Dromedary Droop
▼
Get on hands and knees. Wrists below shoulders, knees below hips. Breathe in — let back stay flat. Breathe out — gently round back up like the letter C, tuck head down. Then release back to flat (not sagging). Rock pelvis gently.
⏱ 8–12 reps · slow and controlled
04Neck Stretch
▼
Sit or stand tall. Slowly drop chin to chest — hold. Return to center. Then turn head right, hold; turn left, hold. Keep shoulders relaxed throughout. Don't force it.
⏱ 2–4 reps each direction · Hold 15–30 sec
05Leg Raise
▼
Sit on a chair, feet hip-width apart. Tighten tummy. Lift one knee to a comfortable height, keeping leg bent. Slowly straighten leg without locking the knee. Lower and repeat. Alternate legs.
⏱ 8–10 reps each leg · 2–3 sets
06Tailor Stretch
▼
Sit on the floor with legs wide in a V shape, back straight. Reach hands forward toward right foot — hold. Return to center. Reach straight forward — hold. Reach toward left foot — hold. Keep hips flat on floor throughout.
⏱ 2–4 reps each direction · Hold 15–30 sec
07Clamming
▼
Lie on your side with a pillow between knees. Keep feet together, bend knees at 90°. Keeping feet touching, rotate top knee upward like a clamshell opening. Don't let your hips roll back. Lower slowly and repeat. Switch sides.
⏱ 10–15 reps each side · 2–3 sets
08Waist Twist
▼
Sit on floor with legs crossed (or on a chair with feet flat). Put left hand on right knee, right hand behind you for support. Slowly twist torso to the right. Hold, return to center. Switch sides.
⏱ 2–4 reps each side · Hold 15–20 sec
09Hip Flexor Stretch
▼
Kneel on right knee, left foot forward at a 90° angle. Keep back straight. Squeeze glutes and shift hips forward gently until you feel a stretch in the front of the right hip/thigh. Hold, then switch sides.
⏱ 3 reps each side · Hold 30 sec
10Triangle Pose
▼
Stand with feet wide apart. Point right foot out, left foot slightly in. Arms out wide. Inhale, then exhale and hinge sideways over the right leg, reaching right hand toward shin/ankle. Left arm reaches up. Hold, then switch sides.
⏱ 3–5 sets each side · Hold 20–30 sec
11Plank
▼
Start in an inclined plank — hands on a stable surface (not floor if it's too hard on wrists). Body in a straight line, core engaged, breathe normally. Don't let hips sag or rise. Slowly lower chest and press back up.
⏱ 5–10 reps · or hold 10–20 sec
12Ball Exercise
▼
Sit on a pregnancy ball with feet hip-width apart, back straight. Gently rock hips side to side or in circles. For chest opener: place ball in a corner, sit back on it, hands behind head, let elbows open outward and breathe deeply.
⏱ 3–5 sets · 30–60 sec per movement
